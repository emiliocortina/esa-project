What's Cooper?

Cooper is a mobile app that presents the data collected by satellites in a simple and undertandable way to the users. All of this data is related to climate change meassure factors. Cooper provides different parameters divided into multiple categories such as :

  • Temperatures
  • Pollution
  • Tide levels
  • Rain

Users will be able to research all this data related to the location they choose, having access to an historical of the variation and other useful aggregate functions such as the average or the deviation. Finally we provide a way to share this data and explain how it affects you, by creating a "Coop" that can be visualized in the explore tab or shared in social networks.

Make the change

Cooper is not a common "Climate change" platform. Our aim is not force you to make the first move, we don't have the right to do that. It's is the moment that you realise what's right and what's not, and you have the power to enforce the goverments to make a change. Cooper is just a source of reliable information, directly provided by the ESA satellites observations.

A law in your city forbide the trafic in the center? Visit Cooper and see if it had a real impact. Factories have increased over the years, have a look at the historial of air pollution meassures.

Now what?

Keep on going, that's it! We've got the support of the ESA (European Space Agency) and we hope that soon more organizations join us on this adventure.

We are planning also to provide a tool for professionals to allow a deeper analysis of the data and also to be able to embed the Coops on your webpage, on a school work, on scientific papers and so on.
